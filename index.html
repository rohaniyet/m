Founder & Visionary

Syed Waqas Asim, known professionally as Waqas Gilani, is the founder and creative force behind Misharfin — a brand that embodies heritage, grace, and modern refinement. His vision redefines luxury perfumery by blending timeless artistry with royal sophistication. Today, Misharfin is emerging as a symbol of British elegance and the favourite choice for luxury connoisseurs across the UK.
